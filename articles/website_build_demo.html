Website Build Demo

This page describes my experience watching and coding along with Edwin in this video.



In this video, Edwin created a simple, responsive landing page with just HTML and CSS. As I followed along, we make the files and adding a few elements. He started off just making a simple navigation bar and adding semantic elements around the links. I was surprised at how fast he moved, but that is probably because he had a plan of what to type. From the start, he used some css elements that I was not familiar with, like justify-content and flex. His design was simple and looked professional.

My experience of this tutorial is mostly positive. It was not hard content, and I understood everything he did and how it affected the website. I felt it was a great beginner's tutorial - he spoke throughout the entire video, there were no long pauses for typing, and he explained everything he typed. It is clear he knows what people want out of these videos. However, I did not like how fast he went. I had to pause many times to keep up with the typing, and that slowed down the process. Though I suppose that could be a fault of my own, not his.

Though I have been learning this, it really demonstrated how so much of the website is the styling. I thought this video was very helpful! I have cards on my website that I could not figure out how to stack vertically when in mobile view, but the flex-direction:column; he used at 35:48 solved my issue! I also saw how he styled his buttons and I copied that for my own website (as seen in the side navigation).

I also got to learn about animations which I have not taken the time to learn, but this was a great introduction that I am excited to implement. Overall, this is one of the few YouTube tutorials I have seen that are actually useful! Perhaps I need to find better people to subscribe to. This guy definitely got my sub.
